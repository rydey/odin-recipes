Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Description

This creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta is a quick and flavorful dish, with rich butter and garlic coating every strand of pasta. Perfect for a simple yet satisfying meal!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup pasta water
  2. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to coat in the garlic butter.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese and heavy cream (if using), adding reserved pasta water as needed for desired consistency.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Top with fresh parsley and serve!
