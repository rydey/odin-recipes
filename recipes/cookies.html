Ingredients

  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Baking Powder
  • Vanilla Essence
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Egg york
  • Dark Chocolate
  • Milk Chocolate
  • A pinch of Salt
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Brown the butter
  2. Let the butter cool down
  3. Add brown sugar and white sugar to the butter in a bowl
  4. Whisk it till it resolves
  5. Mix 1 Egg and Egg york
  6. Add vanilla essence, baking powder and baking soda
  7. Mix well
  8. Add a pinch of salt
  9. Add flour and mix
  10. Add chocolates and keep the mix in the fridge for 30 minutes
  11. Take it out and bake
