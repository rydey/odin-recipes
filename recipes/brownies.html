Brownies Recipe

Description

These rich, fudgy brownies are the perfect balance of gooey and decadent, with a hint of chocolatey sweetness in every bite. Easy to make and irresistibly delicious, they’re sure to be a crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Unsalted Butte, Melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup (90g) chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease an 8x8-inch pan.
  2. Mix melted butter and sugar. Stir in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Add cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder. Mix until combined.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips (optional).
  5. Pour batter into pan and bake for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Let cool before slicing into squares. Enjoy!
